
# Vayva Merchant User Guide

## Welcome to Vayva

Vayva is your all-in-one commerce operating system. This guide covers the basics of managing your store.

## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**: Create an account at `app.vayva.ng`.
2. **Onboarding**: Follow the 10-step wizard to set up your store profile, bank details, and branding.
3. **Verification**: Submit your KYC documents (NIN/BVN) to enable payouts.

## Key Features

### 1. Products & Inventory

- Go to `Products` -> `Add Product`.
- Enable "Track Inventory" to manage stock levels.
- Use `Bulk Edit` for rapid price updates.

### 2. Orders & Fulfillment

- **New Orders**: Appear in `Orders` -> `Open`.
- **Fulfillment**: Click "Fulfill Items" to print packing slips and assign delivery.
- **Delivery Consolidation**: For split orders, fees are automatically unified.

### 3. Finance & Payouts

- **Overview**: View net earnings and pending balances in `Finance`.
- **Withdrawals**: Click "Request Payout". *Note: Minimum N61,000. Requires Verified KYC.*
- **Security**: Large withdrawals require password re-entry.

### 4. Support & Disputes

- **Disputes**: Respond to buyer disputes within 24 hours in the `Disputes` tab.
- **Chat**: Use the built-in chat to talk to customers directly.

## Security Best Practices

- Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
- Never share your password.
- Review audit logs in `Settings` -> `Security` regularly.
